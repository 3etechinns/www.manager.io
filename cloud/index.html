The Power of the Cloud

Discover why it makes sense to use Manager Cloud

Ultra-high performance
All our servers are using high frequency Intel Xeon processors with turbo up to 3.3GHz. This is to make sure Manager runs very fast even if you have larger amount of data.
4 Low Latency Locations
We run servers in 4 indepentent regional data centers - America, Europe, Asia and Oceania. This means working with Manager Cloud will be fast no matter where you are.
Custom domains
We can provide our domain name such as yourbusiness.manager.io or you can run cloud edition on your custom domain name without our branding such as accounting.yourbusiness.com at no extra cost served over a secure HTTPS connection.
Multi-currency pricing
You can pay in your currency to avoid unpredictable bank fees due to foreign currency exchange. No matter where you are, your price will be fixed
No installation, anywhere access
Whether you are on your desktop, laptop, tablet or smartphone. You can access your accounting data from anywhere with a web-browser. No downloads, nothing to install, no conflicting versions or files.
Multi-user access
Share access with your team - so everyone has access to the same numbers at the same time whenever they need it. Accountants and bookkeepers can service you remotely when you allow access to your accounting data.
Access control & user permissions
Set up your users and give them access only to businesses and features they require for their role. Sales team can prepare quotes or issue invoices without having access to any other part of the system. Business associates can be given read-only access to financial statements without being exposed to rest of the system.
Unlimited businesses & users
We don't charge per business or per user. Other cloud accounting solutions will charge you separately for each business you have in cloud. With Manager, you don't get charged extra for multiple businesses.
Automatic upgrades & backups
All updates and upgrades are all done for you automatically, so you'll never worry about having to upgrade again. Your data is completely secure even if your computer is lost or stolen.
No lock-in
Downgrade back to free desktop edition at any point of time in future. Unlike other cloud accounting solutions, with us, you will never lose access to your data when you stop your subscription as you can always download full backup and import into desktop edition for free.

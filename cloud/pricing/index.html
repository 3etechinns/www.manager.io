Can we create as many businesses as we like?

Yes. Unlike most online accounting software, We'll never charge you extra for more businesses in Manager.

Do we pay extra for more users?

No. We don't charge you based on how many users you add. 5 users or 50, the price is the same. If you have many businesses, you can also specify which users can see which businesses.

Will we be charged when our trial is up?

No. We don't ask for your credit card up front, so you'll only be charged when you decide. If you want to continue after your trial, we'll ask for payment details.

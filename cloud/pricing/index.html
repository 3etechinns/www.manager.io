Cloud Pricing FAQ

Some of the more common questions we get.

Can we try Manager Cloud for free?

Of course! Your first 7 days are on us. Sign up for a new Manager Cloud site to get started. If you love it (and we think you will), you can continue using Manager Cloud simply by providing us with your payment details.

Do we pay extra for more users or businesses?

No. We don't charge you based on how many users or businesses you add. 5 or 50, the price is the same. If you have many businesses, you can also specify which businesses are visible to which users. Great for accountants and bookkeepers with many clients.

What payment options do you accept?

We accept MasterCard, Visa, and American Express. For customers purchasing an annual subscription, we also accept bank transfers or PayPal.

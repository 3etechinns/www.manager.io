In Manager, you can customize the date format to match your culture or local business practices. Date options include day, month, and year format and order.

In the Preferences tab, click the Date Format link:

From the dropdown box that appears, choose your format and click Update.

Note
Date format is a global preference affecting all businesses. In some prior versions of the program, date formats could be set individually, by business.

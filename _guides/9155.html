In Manager, you can set the number format to match your culture or local business practices. Number format options include digit grouping, decimal mark, and group size.

In the Preferences tab, click Number Format link:

=>

In the dropdown box on the screen that appears, choose your format and click Update:

Note
Number formats apply globally to all businesses created in Manager. In some previous versions of the program, they could be set individually, by business.
